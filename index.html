Development Blog

Updates On The Studio, Team, and Projects

4/6/2023

This past month the team has offically decided on a name for our upcoming project...."Archasm". Since the last update not much has happened on the side of development, the team took a week long break. We started to work on enemies to be added to the game, as of right now we have a goblin, illustrated in the image above by Jack Thompson, that spawns in and runs towards the player. We are working on a health and attacking system for the goblin. We implemented an inventory system as of right now it only contains swords but ost importantly it saves the players inventory per game session. We are excited to see what we come up with for next months update! -K.S

3/9/2023

Today we presented a playable demo (to our current liking) to show where we are right now in our project. We still have a long way to go but with only a month of work put in so far we are excited to see what the next month will bring us. -K.S

2/6/2023

The project has been created!!! Our project will be a 2D pixel-based survival game, the player will be able to battle monsters, collect loot and mine rare ores. The team has decided that the engine we will use for our project is Godot 3.5. Godot speciallizes in the creation of 2D and 3D games with the engine not being very taxing on the teams computers. -K.S

1/26/2023

Initial Commit! The team has been created consisting of Kevin, Forest, Joel and Jack. Not much has happened as for project development, but atleast the team is formulated. -K.S
